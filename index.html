Brave Trails Sexual Assault Awareness

What is it?

Sexual assault is the act of pressuring, manipulating, or forcing a non-consenting person into doing sexual acts. Sexual assault isn't just committed by strangers, it could also be your friend, teacher, family, partners, or anyone else. Sexual harassment is the act of verbally saying things that are sexually explicit in nature to a non-consenting person.

Common Signs

Common signs of sexual abuse or assault are things as such as
•They tell you to keep the abuse a 'secret'
•They act like the abuse is a 'game'
•They touch you without consent
•They treat you like your 'special'
•They get angry when you say no to intercourse / sexual acts. ex: hit you, yell at you, guilt trip you
•Lovebombing
•Committing sexual acts while you're incapacitated

Consentual Non-Consent

Consentual non consent is sexual roleplay that appears to be non-consenting but is. This can be absolutely be a form of sexual play. CNC is pre-consenting sex where both parties consent, set boundaries, and plan out the interaction beforehand. Unless consent is taken away during the sexual interaction, then this is a form of consentual sex.

How to Take Accountibility

If you realize reading this you may have sexually assaulted someone and want to make amends, how would you go about that? Step one is definitely take accountability and apologize. Keep the apology based on how you made them feel! Step two is accept if they don't accept. You hurt someone and they do not need to forgive you.
