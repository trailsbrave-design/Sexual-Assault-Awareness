Common Signs

Common signs of sexual abuse or assault are things as such as, •They tell you to keep the abuse a 'secret' •They act like the abuse is a 'game'. •They touch you without consent •They treat you like your 'special' •They get angry when you say no to intercourse / sexual acts. ex: hit you, yell at you, guilt trip you. •Lovebombing •Committing sexual acts while your incapacitated.

Consentual Non-Consent

Consentual non consent is sexual roleplay that seems to not be not consenting but is. This can be absolutely be a form of sexual play. CNC is pre-consenting sex where both parties consent, set boundaries, and plan out the interaction beforehand. Unless consent is taken away during the sexual interaction, then this is a form of consetual sex.

How to Take Acountibility

If you realize reading this you may have sexually assaulted someone and want to make amends, how would you go about that? Step one is definitely take accountability and apologize. Keep the apology based on how you made them feel! Step two is accept if they don't accept. You hurt someone and they do not need to forgive you.
